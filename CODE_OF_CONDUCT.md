# Code of Conduct

AgriConto Pro aims to be a respectful, professional and collaborative open-source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give clear technical feedback.
- Welcome contributors with different experience levels.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information.
- Sharing secrets, credentials or sensitive business data.
- Intentionally disrupting project work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide or remove comments, issues, pull requests or contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to being blocked from the project.
