# Product

AgriConto Pro is an Italian agricultural management platform for farms, commercialisti and consulenti del lavoro. It organizes accounting, workforce, crop, document and report data so professionals can review cleaner records.

## Vision

Make farm administration easier to understand, verify and share without pretending to replace official advisors or filing systems.

## Target Users

- Farm owners and small agricultural businesses.
- Commercialisti who need cleaner expense, sales and document packs.
- Consulenti del lavoro who review workers, workdays and wage-related summaries.
- Cooperatives or agritech operators that need structured farm data.

## Pain Points

- Farm records are often split across paper, chat messages, spreadsheets and invoices.
- Accountants receive incomplete or late documentation.
- Worker workdays and balances are hard to reconcile.
- Crop profitability is difficult to estimate without linked cost and sales data.

## Value Proposition

AgriConto Pro creates a farm-scoped operational record that is permission-protected, auditable, reportable and exportable.

## Core Modules

- Farms and farm members.
- Workers and workdays.
- Fields and crops.
- Expenses and sales.
- Suppliers and customers.
- Documents and document requests.
- Reports and Pacchetto Commercialista.
- Statistiche e Analisi.
- Desktop app foundation for Debian/Linux.

## What It Does

- Organizes business records by farm.
- Helps users find missing documents.
- Generates management reports and PDF exports.
- Provides analytics for revenue, costs, labor, crops and documents.
- Preserves audit history for important actions.

## What It Does Not Do

- It does not replace a commercialista or consulente del lavoro.
- It does not submit official filings.
- It does not implement official tax, IVA, payroll, INPS or INAIL calculations.
- It does not claim legal or fiscal compliance.

## Commercialista Value

- Cleaner expense and sales records.
- Missing document visibility.
- Accountant pack export.
- Read-only access model for financial records.

## Farmer Value

- Faster daily work recording.
- Centralized documents.
- Clear worker balances.
- Crop and field visibility.

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term roadmap includes offline/PWA field workflows, OCR invoice capture, AI-assisted document review, cloud collaboration and a multi-farm accountant workspace.
